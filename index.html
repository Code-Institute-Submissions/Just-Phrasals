What are Phrasals Verbs?

A phrasal verb is a verb expression made of a verb and a preposition where the meaning is not always obvious, for example “give up” (give + up).

Adding the preposition completely changes the meaning of the root verb. For example you probably know what “work” means but what about “work out”? You probably know what “give” means but what about “give up”?
